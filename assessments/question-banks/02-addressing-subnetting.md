# Q11-Q20: Addressing and Subnetting

11. Q: Usable hosts in /27?
A: 30.

12. Q: Why use VLSM?
A: Efficiently allocate different subnet sizes.

13. Q: What causes overlapping subnet problems?
A: Ambiguous forwarding decisions.

14. Q: Purpose of route summarization?
A: Reduce routing table size and churn.

15. Q: Mandatory IPv6 address type on interface?
A: Link-local.

16. Q: If host ARPs remote IP directly, likely issue?
A: Wrong subnet mask.

17. Q: /31 commonly used for?
A: Point-to-point links.

18. Q: What does dual-stack require during validation?
A: Separate IPv4 and IPv6 verification.

19. Q: Why reserve address growth space?
A: Avoid disruptive renumbering later.

20. Q: Fast way to detect duplicate IP?
A: ARP instability and conflicting neighbor entries.
