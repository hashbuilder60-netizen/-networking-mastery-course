# Q71-Q80: Cloud, SDN, Hybrid

71. Q: Cloud equivalent of segmentation boundary?
A: Subnets plus security policies.

72. Q: Why avoid overlapping CIDRs in hybrid design?
A: Breaks deterministic routing.

73. Q: SG vs NACL key difference?
A: SG stateful, NACL stateless.

74. Q: Overlay network purpose?
A: Logical segmentation over shared underlay.

75. Q: Common hybrid outage cause?
A: Missing return route.

76. Q: Transit hub benefit?
A: Centralized route and policy control.

77. Q: What must be validated besides route?
A: Security policy and DNS path.

78. Q: Why MTU planning in tunnels?
A: Encapsulation overhead can break large payloads.

79. Q: Cloud route exists but app still fails, next check?
A: Security rules and return path.

80. Q: SDN operational advantage?
A: Consistent policy automation at scale.
