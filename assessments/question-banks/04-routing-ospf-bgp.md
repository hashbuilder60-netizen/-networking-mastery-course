# Q31-Q40: Routing, OSPF, BGP

31. Q: Route selection starts with?
A: Longest prefix match.

32. Q: Administrative distance used for?
A: Choosing between routing sources.

33. Q: OSPF neighbor stuck EXSTART often indicates?
A: MTU mismatch.

34. Q: OSPF requires area match?
A: Yes, adjacency depends on it.

35. Q: Why use floating static route?
A: Backup path when primary fails.

36. Q: Why can route exist but app fail?
A: Return path, policy, or service issue.

37. Q: BGP compared to OSPF is primarily?
A: Policy-driven inter-domain routing.

38. Q: Benefit of area summarization?
A: Less flooding and better scalability.

39. Q: First OSPF validation command?
A: `show ip ospf neighbor`.

40. Q: Default route role at branch?
A: Forward unknown destinations toward upstream edge.
