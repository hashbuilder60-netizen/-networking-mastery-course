# Q91-Q100: Design and Career Readiness

91. Q: Core design objective?
A: Reliable, secure, operable architecture.

92. Q: Why bound failure domains?
A: Limit outage spread.

93. Q: What is overengineering risk?
A: Operational complexity and slower recovery.

94. Q: Why architecture decision records?
A: Preserve rationale for future changes.

95. Q: What makes portfolio strong?
A: Business goal, implementation, verification, outcomes.

96. Q: Interview outage answer should include?
A: Method, evidence, fix, prevention.

97. Q: Why timed practical drills?
A: Build decision quality under pressure.

98. Q: Certification prep mistake?
A: Theory without repeated hands-on practice.

99. Q: Resume bullet should show?
A: Measurable impact.

100. Q: Best long-term growth strategy?
A: Continuous practice across networking, security, automation, and cloud.
