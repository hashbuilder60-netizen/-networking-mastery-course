# Q21-Q30: Switching, VLAN, STP

21. Q: Why create VLANs?
A: Segment broadcast domains and enforce policy boundaries.

22. Q: Purpose of trunk links?
A: Carry multiple VLANs between switches.

23. Q: Why not allow all VLANs everywhere?
A: Increases blast radius and security exposure.

24. Q: Why is STP required?
A: Prevent Layer 2 loops.

25. Q: STP root placement should be where?
A: Intentional core or distribution layer.

26. Q: EtherChannel main benefit?
A: Redundancy plus aggregated bandwidth.

27. Q: LACP mismatch symptom?
A: Member ports suspended or bundle not formed.

28. Q: Native VLAN mismatch impact?
A: Unexpected untagged traffic behavior.

29. Q: What does frequent topology change imply?
A: Instability or loop risk.

30. Q: Best edge-port safety feature?
A: BPDU Guard.
