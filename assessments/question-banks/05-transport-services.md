# Q41-Q50: Transport and Services

41. Q: TCP handshake sequence?
A: SYN, SYN-ACK, ACK.

42. Q: UDP preferred when?
A: Low latency matters more than retransmission.

43. Q: What does TCP RST indicate?
A: Session actively refused/reset.

44. Q: Ping works but web fails likely due?
A: Port/service/policy issue.

45. Q: DHCP relay purpose?
A: Forward DHCP broadcasts across routed networks.

46. Q: DNS SERVFAIL indicates?
A: Resolver-side processing failure.

47. Q: PAT advantage?
A: Many clients share one public IP.

48. Q: Why validate NTP early?
A: Time affects logs, auth, and certificates.

49. Q: QoS can do what?
A: Prioritize traffic under congestion.

50. Q: QoS cannot do what?
A: Create physical bandwidth.
