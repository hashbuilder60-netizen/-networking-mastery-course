# Q51-Q60: Wireless and Security

51. Q: High RSSI but poor throughput likely cause?
A: Interference or airtime contention.

52. Q: Why separate guest SSID VLAN?
A: Isolation from internal resources.

53. Q: 802.1X provides?
A: Identity-based network access control.

54. Q: ACL processing order?
A: Top-down first match wins.

55. Q: Common ACL outage cause?
A: Shadow deny above permit.

56. Q: DHCP snooping protects against?
A: Rogue DHCP and spoofed bindings.

57. Q: DAI depends on?
A: DHCP snooping binding table.

58. Q: Why secure management plane?
A: Prevent unauthorized device control.

59. Q: Evidence ACL works correctly?
A: Expected permit and deny test outcomes with counters.

60. Q: Minimum hardening trio?
A: SSH, AAA, and centralized logging.
