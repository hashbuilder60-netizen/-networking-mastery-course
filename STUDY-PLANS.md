# Study Plans

## 12-Week Intensive
- Weekly hours: 12-18
- Best for: Full-time learners or bootcamp pace
- Strategy: 1-2 modules per week + weekend labs

## 20-Week Standard
- Weekly hours: 6-10
- Best for: Working professionals
- Strategy: 1 module per week + cumulative monthly review

## 32-Week Part-Time
- Weekly hours: 4-6
- Best for: Beginners balancing school/work
- Strategy: Slow pace, heavy lab repetition, frequent quizzes

## Daily Session Template (90 Minutes)
- 20 min: Read module theory
- 45 min: Hands-on lab
- 15 min: Troubleshooting challenge
- 10 min: Journal + flashcards

## Progress Tracking
- Mark each lesson complete in module checklists.
- Keep a troubleshooting journal with root-cause notes.
- Retake quizzes below 80% score.
- Redo labs from memory every 3 modules.
